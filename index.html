Smoovee
Kitchen · E-commerce · USA

Brand-new product launched cold in the US. Lookalikes, UGC creatives, and bid caps on Meta, with Google as demand capture. The product sold out.

11.57x Google ROAS 117 purchases · 1 ad set Sold out
Full story
The challenge

Launching a brand-new product in the US with no audience and no brand recognition. Find winning audiences and creatives fast, control CPP, and build a full-funnel structure from scratch.

The strategy

Parallel Meta campaigns with Lookalike Audiences, UGC-style creatives, and bid cap strategies to control CPP. Google Ads added as demand capture for branded and category search. Mother's Day used as a seasonal scaling angle.

The result

Google Ads at 11.57x pixel ROAS, 2.52x blended across all channels. Best Meta ad set: 117 purchases at $49.70 CPP. Products drove to sold-out status, with live orders tracked in TripleWhale.

Eric Rich
High-Ticket Coaching · USA

$8K program sold to cold US traffic through the "Income Seeker" VSL series: free webinar, retargeting, call booking. Stable funnel at $2,000/day.

547 calls booked $4.87 best CPL 1,003 webinar signups
Full story
The challenge

An $8K high-ticket real estate and surplus funds program in the US market. Selling $8K directly to cold traffic burned budget with no return.

The diagnosis

Without an educational VSL at the top of the funnel, leads arrived too cold for a call. Cost per booking was unsustainable and close rates were low.

The strategy

Built the "Income Seeker" VSL series (Surplus Funds, Unclaimed Money, Government Glitch) as top-of-funnel entry. Best VSL drove signups at $4.87. Free webinar → retargeting → call booking → $8K offer.

The result

$34K invested in call booking campaigns in April generated 547 booked calls and 1,003 webinar signups: a stable, scalable funnel at $2,000/day.

Pick My Brain
AI SaaS · Silicon Valley

Early-stage SaaS with zero audience. Awareness first, then aggressive retargeting of engaged users. CAC dropped as the funnel warmed up.

0 → 500 subscribers 2,175 signups · 6 months 10.86K MAU
Full story
The challenge

Pick My Brain launched with no user base, entering an extremely competitive AI platform market in the US.

The diagnosis

In early-stage SaaS, converting cold traffic directly to subscriptions rarely works. Awareness and authority had to come first.

The strategy

Two-step funnel: awareness campaigns with valuable content, followed by aggressive retargeting of engaged users. CAC dropped as the funnel warmed up.

The result

From zero to 500 paid subscriptions with consistent month-over-month growth across 3 international markets, with the USA as the primary market.

MOSSY
Fashion · E-commerce · Brazil

Validated product stuck at R$30K/month: ROAS dropped every time budget grew. Full funnel rebuild with stage-specific creatives unlocked scale.

R$30K → R$320K/mo 10x ROAS held at scale
Full story
The context

MOSSY had a validated product, but ROAS dropped every time they increased budget. The campaign structure could not scale.

The diagnosis

The account did not separate cold traffic from retargeting. The algorithm mixed audiences and cost per purchase kept rising.

The strategy

Full rebuild with top, mid, and bottom of funnel. Stage-specific creatives allowed scaling without hurting ROAS.

Consistent 10x ROAS with a 10x larger budget. The brand gained revenue predictability and real scaling capacity: R$30K to R$320K/month.

Bloom → Loum
Fashion · Rebranding · BR/PT

Complete rebrand launched without losing the existing base: retention retargeting in parallel with acquisition for the new brand.

19x ROAS R$686K in conversions
Full story
The rebrand

Bloom became Loum: complete rebrand, new identity, new name. The challenge was launching without losing the existing base while scaling sales.

The risk

Double exposure: losing existing customers during the transition and not building a new audience fast enough.

The strategy

The result

19x ROAS, the best in the portfolio, with R$686,000 in conversions recorded by Meta Ads and the new brand established in Brazil and Portugal.

Traffic Raptors
Nutraceuticals · US/CA/EU/AU

Four countries running simultaneously, each with its own structure, creatives, and copy adapted to the local market.

3.4x to 5.3x ROAS 4 markets at once
Full story
The challenge

Running nutraceutical campaigns in the US, Canada, Europe, and Australia simultaneously, each market with its own rules and distinct audiences.

The diagnosis

Generic creatives ignored cultural differences. What worked in one market did not convert in another.

The strategy

Separate structure per market, with creatives and copy adapted for each country and systematic testing to find the specific conversion angles.

The result

Consistent ROAS between 3.4x and 5.3x across all 4 countries. Sustainable scale because each market had its own optimized structure.
